Services

What I Do?

Counseling for Anxiety

When it comes to treating anxiety disorders, research shows that therapy is usually the most effective option. That's because therapy for anxiety — as opposed to taking anxiety medication — treats more than just symptoms to the problem.

Individual Therapy

If you've been feeling hopeless or lost lately, or struggling with a problem in your life that feels unsurmountable, individual therapy could help you improve your everyday life.

Life Transitions

There's no denying that life often presents us with our fair share of challenges, usually in the form of sudden change. These life transitions, even the positive ones, can throw us into a tailspin and leave us feeling stressed, confused, and vulnerable.

PTSD

Posttraumatic stress disorder — also known as PTSD — is a mental health challenge that may occur in individuals who have experienced or witnessed a traumatic event such as a natural disaster, a terrorist act, an act of war, a serious accident, rape, or any other violent personal assault.

Telehealth

My telehealth solution allows you to schedule an appointment and have the entire therapy session take place digitally. While you don’t need a video camera, I do recommend you use one. To learn more and get started with virtual therapy, please contact me today.

Therapy for Depression

I want you to know that you are not alone and there are people who can help. Many of my clients find relief in realizing that their struggle does not own them and that there are many options available for them to once again have hope for their lives.

My Skills

What i’m good?

I am a Clinical Psychologist currently pursuing a Doctor of Psychology in Clinical Psychology. My primary target focuses on mental health especially of emerging adults and people affected by cancer.
I have a strong interest in promoting mental health awareness among Kenyans. As a result, my impact nationally has been felt having worked with Edmund Rice Advocacy Network advocating for Child protection rights programs. I have also worked with KCCL (Kenya Community Centre for Learning) as a life skills trainer for special needs children in 2016. In the same year I worked as a Counseling and forensic child psychiatry and outpatient at Mathari Hospital Rehab Centre. In 2017, I had the chance to work with an autistic child as a Behavior Specialist at Terramoyo International School.
My experience with Cancer Patients came from 2018 at Faraja. I also provided Psychoeducation to parents whose children are affected by cancer at KNH. In the same year I was at USIU counseling Centre for students. In the year 2019, I worked with Young Professionals on Mental Health for emerging adults and was also a panelist on suicide and depression at the Kilifi New Year University.
In 2020, I acted as a supervisor for Masters and Undergraduate Students at USIU while also undertaking my Doctoral Internship at Faraja. I was also a facilitator at Kipepeo Support Group which is a Grieve and Bereavement Support Group.
